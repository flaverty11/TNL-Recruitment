Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

1. Who we are

The Next Level ("we", "us", "our") helps young soccer athletes connect with US college coaches and pursue athletic scholarships. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and Coach Contacts service, and how it's used.

2. Information we collect

When you submit our application form, purchase coach contact data, or otherwise contact us, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and country
  • Age, playing position, and athletic/academic details you choose to share
  • Payment information processed securely by Stripe (we never store full card numbers)
  • Basic technical data such as browser type, for site functionality

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to evaluate your athletic profile, contact you about our services, process payments, and — where you've engaged our recruitment packages — to represent you to college coaches on your behalf.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with:

  • College coaches and recruiting staff, where you've engaged a recruitment package that requires outreach on your behalf
  • Stripe, for secure payment processing
  • Service providers strictly necessary to operate our website and email communications

5. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal requirements. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at tnlrecruitment@outlook.com.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to tnlrecruitment@outlook.com.
