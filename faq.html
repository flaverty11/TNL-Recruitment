Everything you need to know about scholarships, our process, and how we work with athletes and families.

Getting Started
We work with athletes aged 14–20 who are playing at club, academy, or school level and are serious about competing in the United States. There's no need to already be "elite" — coaches recruit potential as much as current ability, and our free evaluation will tell you honestly where you stand.
After you submit the application form, one of our advisors reviews your footage, academic record, and goals, then gives you an honest assessment of which divisions (NCAA D1/D2/D3, NAIA, NJCAA) realistically fit your profile — before you pay anything.
Most athletes see their first offers within 2–4 months of completing their profile and highlight reel, though this varies by division, position, and intake timing. The earlier you start relative to your intended enrollment year, the more options you'll have.
Pricing & Packages
Prospect (£450) covers your profile, CV, and highlight reel build. Contender (£1,000) adds direct coach outreach across our network. Elite Pro (£1,350) is our full-service package including negotiation support, paperwork handling, and ongoing advisor access until you sign. Full breakdowns are on our pricing page.
No agency can ethically guarantee a scholarship — final decisions sit with individual coaches and universities. What we guarantee is the work: professional outreach, a polished athletic profile, and direct access to coaches who are actively recruiting in your position and division.
It's a self-serve alternative to full recruitment support — a verified list of coach emails for a specific division (NCAA, NAIA, or NJCAA), so you can run your own outreach campaign. See Coach Contacts for current pricing per division.
